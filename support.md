# Radish — Support

Need a hand with Radish? Email **[coleocofer@gmail.com](mailto:coleocofer@gmail.com)** and I'll get
back to you. You can also send feedback right from the app: **Settings → Send Feedback**.

## Common questions

**I bought Radish but it's locked on a new device.**
Open **Settings → Restore Purchases** (or the Restore button on the unlock screen). Your purchase is
tied to your Apple ID, so it restores for free on any device signed into the same account.

**How does the free trial work?**
You get full access free for 14 days. After that the app becomes read-only — your data stays
visible, and a single one-time purchase unlocks logging again. There's no subscription.

**Where is my data stored?**
On your device. If you turn on iCloud Sync (Settings), it's also kept in your own private iCloud so it
stays in step across your devices. It's never sent to a server I control.

**How do I delete my data?**
Delete individual entries in the app, or delete the app to remove everything stored locally. If you
used iCloud Sync, you can also remove the data from your iCloud account settings.

**A barcode won't scan or a food is missing.**
Barcode and branded-food lookups use the Open Food Facts database and need an internet connection.
Generic foods are searched from a database built into the app and work offline. If something's wrong
or missing, send feedback and I'll take a look.

**Does it work on Apple Watch?**
Yes — there's an Apple Watch app for logging food and weight, plus Siri logging.
